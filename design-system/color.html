Volt is this was made in 2026

Electric lime is the one tech note in a warm, earthy room. It has two legibility modes — and they are NOT interchangeable. Bright lime glows on dark; on kraft it's illegible as text, so it drops to --volt-deep for lines and links.

Why kraft as base

Warm, tactile, analog — it reads as a real travel journal, not a UI. The bold colors land harder against a calm earthy ground.

Why two greens

--pitch is the footy/action green; --globe-green matches the literal land on the 3D globe. Two jobs, one family — that's the fusion.

Why volt has two tokens

The bright lime glows on navy but vanishes as text on kraft. --volt-deep is the legible-on-light twin. One spark, two surfaces.

Why navy is shared

The dark sections reuse the exact globe navy so the whole site feels carved from the same object as the interactive globe.
