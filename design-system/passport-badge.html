Usage

Place a stamp badge on a destination / country-story card the moment a country flips to marked visited. The variant encodes meaning: a circular stamp-red for a normal entry, the ocean ADMITTED box for a border crossing, the gold oval reserved for a once-in-a-lifetime stop (a match at the Bernabéu), and the scalloped edge for far-flung passport pages. Lay 2–4 at varied rotations so a well-travelled page reads as collage, not catalog. The badge is the static mark; the live stamp-thunk motion (overshoot, --ease-thunk) plays once on first visit.
