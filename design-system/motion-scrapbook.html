Why this motion language? Every interaction has mass — things drop and settle rather than slide. --ease-thunk carries the overshoot (tape slap, stamp, pin) so paper feels pressed; --ease-paper carries the smooth develop/peel. All of it shares the globe's spring (stiffness 300 / damping 25) so the scrapbook and the 3D globe move as one system.
