01 Ticket Button ★ Signature Primary

The match-ticket / boarding-pass stub — the signature motif of the whole system. Punched notches at the seam, a dashed tear line, Space Mono label. It is the loudest CTA on the page — the "I'm going" button — and it recurs as the nav (boarding pass), tags, and the back-to-globe control. Why mono: tickets and boarding passes are always set in monospace, so the form tells you it's a stub before you read a word.

02 Stamp Button Secondary

A rubber passport stamp: octagon, --stamp-red double border, rotated −3°, multiply-blended onto the paper so it reads as pressed ink — not a flat vector. Used for collect / save / "mark visited" actions. Why multiply: it lets the kraft grain show through the red, the single trick that sells "real stamp."

03 Ghost / Link Tertiary

The quiet option: Plus Jakarta label, no fill, a single --volt-deep underline. This is THE spark — the one electric-lime note per composition (the bright --volt is illegible as a line on kraft, so the deep variant carries it). Hover grows the underline left→right.
