The type system is a fusion — stadium banner, boarding-pass data, and margin scribble living on the same kraft page. Each family does one job and never the others. That discipline is what makes the collage read as sharp, not noisy.

Why Anton. It's the loudest voice on the page — a vinyl stadium banner draped over the kraft. Reserve it for the moment that should shout: a country name, a scoreline header, the jersey number on a player card. Numerals are wide and confident, built for scoreboards.
