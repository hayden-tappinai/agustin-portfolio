tear here to board ✈ — every link is a destination

The round notches + dashed line + "tear here ✂" hint sell the perforation and foreshadow the ticket rip-off — the system's hero coded interaction (SPEC §7.1): clicking a ticket/stub splits it along the seam (--ease-thunk on the split, --ease-paper on the fall, --t-tear ~520ms). The globe already owns the hover-lift motion, so the nav stays still until you commit to a route.

Space Mono everywhere because monospace is the literal typography of real tickets, gate signs and scoreboards — it does the "official document" work so the warm kraft stock and route dashes can carry the personality. Generous negative space inside each field keeps it sharp and current, never craft-fair.
