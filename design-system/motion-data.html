Why volt once
The single electric-lime spark lands on the live readout — the active dot, the count-up, the pulsing pin. One spark, never two competing.
Why Space Mono
Mono = the data voice: coordinates, stats, ticket fields, scoreboard digits. It makes numbers feel measured and tabular, like a flight board.
Why one motion clock
Route-draw and count-up ride --ease-draw and the 300/25 spring — the globe owns the hover-lift, so the coded UI moves with the centerpiece, not against it.
